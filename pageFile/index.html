About Me

Detail-Oriented QA Professional

I am a passionate Quality Assurance Engineer with over 3 years of experience in software testing and quality assurance. I specialize in aligning product requirements with UI/UX design, creating comprehensive test strategies, implementing automation frameworks, and ensuring products meet the highest quality standards prior to release.

In my current role, I also serve as a QA Supervisor, leading a team of five QA engineers. I plan and assign daily testing tasks, guide team members based on defined testing strategies, and ensure execution stays aligned with project timelines and quality goals. I actively review test results, provide feedback, and help resolve blockers to maintain consistent delivery and team performance.

My approach combines strong analytical skills with technical expertise to identify defects early in the development lifecycle, helping reduce costs and improve overall product reliability. I thrive in collaborative environments, working closely with development teams to embed quality into products from the ground up.
